Early Life and Career

  • Virat Kohli is a prominent Indian cricketer known for his exceptional batting skills, particularly in limited-overs formats, and his aggressive captaincy style. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he has become one of the most successful and celebrated cricketers globally. Kohli is renowned for his fitness, technique, and ability to chase down targets, setting numerous records in both ODIs and T20Is.
  • He began playing cricket at a young age and joined the West Delhi Cricket Academy at nine. He captained India's victorious Under-19 World Cup team in 2008. Kohli made his debut for Delhi in domestic cricket at 18 and his ODI debut for India against Sri Lanka in the same year.

Education

  • Virat Kohli is a 12th grade pass; he completed his schooling but did not pursue higher education. He studied at Vishal Bharti Public School and Savior Convent School in Delhi. He left formal education after completing class 12 to focus on his burgeoning cricket career, particularly after being selected for the India Under-19 team.
  • From a young age, he showed a strong interest in cricket, often picking up a bat instead of books. Recognizing his talent, his family enrolled him in the West Delhi Cricket Academy when he was just nine years old. As his cricketing schedule became more demanding, he later shifted to Saviour Convent School in Paschim Vihar to balance both studies and sports. Although academics took a backseat due to his intense focus on cricket, Kohli was known to be sharp and disciplined in school. His teachers and coaches remember him as a bright student who managed to stay attentive despite dedicating most of his time to training. Personal Life

  • Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi to Prem Kohli, a criminal lawyer, and Saroj Kohli, a homemaker. He grew up in a close-knit Punjabi family with an elder brother, Vikas, and an elder sister, Bhawna.
  • Virat is known for being emotionally attached to his family, especially after the passing of his father in 2006, which was a turning point in his life. In December 2017, he married famous Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in a private ceremony in Italy.
  • The couple is considered one of the most admired celebrity pairs in India. They were blessed with a daughter, Vamika, in January 2021, and a baby boy, Akaay, in February 2024. Kohli is also known for his disciplined lifestyle, strong fitness routine, and love for animals. Outside cricket, he enjoys traveling, music, and spending quality time with his family. Despite his fame, he remains humble and grounded in his personal life.
